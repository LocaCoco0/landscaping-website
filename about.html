About Marquez General Services

Marquez General Services began nearly 26 years ago when our founder started with one simple job — removing trees for local homeowners. Over time, his skills, dedication, and passion for outdoor work grew into full landscaping and yard‑care services.

As the years went on, he expanded his expertise and earned his certification in home maintenance. This allowed the company to offer a wider range of services, from landscaping and cleaning to full home repair and upkeep.

What has never changed is his commitment to fair pricing, honest work, and leaving every job with a clean, professional finish. Our customers trust us because we treat every home like our own — with care, respect, and attention to detail.
